About Baguio City

Baguio City is known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines because of its cool weather and beautiful mountain scenery. It is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country.

Famous Tourist Spots

Burnham Park

One of the most visited tourist spots in Baguio City.

Mines View Park

Offers a beautiful mountain view and cool climate.

Camp John Hay

A relaxing place with pine trees and nature.
