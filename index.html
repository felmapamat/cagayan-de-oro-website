The City of Golden Friendship

About the City

Cagayan de Oro City is one of the fastest-growing cities in Mindanao. It is known as the City of Golden Friendship because of the kindness and hospitality of its people. The city is located in the province of Misamis Oriental in the Philippines and is famous for tourism, business, and adventure activities.


The city is also known for its white-water rafting adventure, beautiful tourist spots, and exciting festivals. Many students, tourists, and visitors come to Cagayan de Oro every year because of its friendly environment and modern lifestyle.

Tourist Spots

White Water Rafting

One of the most popular tourist activities in Cagayan de Oro. Visitors from different places enjoy the exciting rafting adventure.

Mapawa Nature Park

A beautiful nature park where you can enjoy hiking, waterfalls, and outdoor activities with friends and family.

Macahambus Cave

A famous historical and natural attraction in the city known for its beautiful cave and hanging bridge.

Culture and Festival

Cagayan de Oro celebrates the Higalaay Festival every August. The festival is a celebration of friendship, culture, and unity. Many people enjoy street dancing, parades, and cultural performances during the festival.


The city is also known for its delicious food, friendly people, and rich culture. Visitors always feel welcome in the City of Golden Friendship.
