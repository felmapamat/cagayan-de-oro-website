About Baguio City

Baguio City is known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines because of its cool weather and beautiful mountain scenery. It is located in the province of Benguet and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the country.


The city is famous for its pine trees, parks, and tourist spots such as Burnham Park, Mines View Park, and Camp John Hay. Many visitors travel to Baguio every year to relax, enjoy the cool climate, and experience its culture.

Famous Tourist Spots

Burnham Park

Burnham Park is the most famous park in Baguio City. Visitors can enjoy boating, biking, and relaxing in the cool weather.

Mines View Park

Mines View Park offers a beautiful view of the mountains and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the city.

Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay is known for its pine trees, cool weather, and relaxing environment. It is perfect for nature lovers.

Culture and Festivals

Baguio City celebrates the Panagbenga Festival every February. The festival is famous for its flower floats, street dancing, and colorful celebrations.


The city is also known for fresh strawberries, local handicrafts, and friendly people. Visitors always enjoy the culture and traditions of Baguio City.
