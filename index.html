About Baguio City

Baguio City is known as the Summer Capital of the Philippines because of its cool climate, beautiful mountain scenery, and tourist attractions such as Burnham Park, Mines View Park, and Camp John Hay. It is one of the most visited cities in the Philippines.

Famous Tourist Spots

Burnham Park

Burnham Park is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Baguio City. Visitors can enjoy boating, biking, and relaxing in the park.

Mines View Park

Mines View Park offers a beautiful mountain view and cool climate. It is one of the most visited places in Baguio.

Camp John Hay

Camp John Hay is known for its pine trees, cool weather, and relaxing environment. It is a perfect place for tourists.
